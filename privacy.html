PomoTime Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 22, 2026

Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to PomoTime ("we", "our", "us"), a Pomodoro timer and productivity application developed by SLaboratory. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using PomoTime, you agree to the terms described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the application.

2. Information We Collect

PomoTime values privacy and aims to minimize data collection. We collect the following categories of data to provide and improve our services:

A. Service Diagnostics and Usage (Third-Party Providers)

We integrate several trusted industry-standard SDKs to monitor the app's performance, stability, and subscription services:

  • Firebase Analytics (Google LLC): We collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics (e.g., button clicks, feature usage, session duration) to understand how the app is used and improve the interface. No personally identifiable information (PII) is sent to Firebase Analytics.
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Google LLC): In the event of an app crash, we collect device-specific diagnostic information (such as operating system version, device model, and crash logs) to diagnose and fix software bugs. This data is strictly anonymous.
  • RevenueCat, Inc.: To manage premium in-app purchases and subscriptions, we process purchase history, transaction IDs, and subscription status. This data is associated with an anonymous app-user ID to verify your premium access.

B. Third-Party Integration (Spotify SDK)

If you choose to link your Spotify account within PomoTime, the app uses the official Spotify App Remote SDK. This integration allows you to control audio playback from within the PomoTime application while working or resting.

  • We do not store or transmit your Spotify login credentials. All authentication is handled securely and directly by Spotify.
  • We do not collect or monitor your listening history or library.

C. Local Data Storage

Most of your data is stored strictly on your own device and never leaves it. This includes:

  • Timer settings, configurations, and completed intervals.
  • Tasks and notes associated with your focus sessions.
  • Local daily, weekly, and monthly productivity statistics.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To ensure the core functionality of the timer, tasks, and notifications.
  • To verify, process, and maintain your premium subscriptions via RevenueCat.
  • To monitor app performance and fix technical stability issues using Firebase Crashlytics.
  • To continuously refine and optimize our user interface and experience based on Firebase Analytics.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share information with trusted third-party service providers (Google Firebase and RevenueCat) as described in Section 2, solely for the operational purposes of the application. These providers are bound by strict privacy policies and data protection regulations.

5. Security of Your Data

We take the security of your data seriously. We use commercially acceptable methods to safeguard the minor diagnostic and subscription information we process. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

PomoTime is a general productivity tool and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so that we can perform necessary deletions.

7. GDPR and international Users

If you reside in the European Union (EU) or European Economic Area (EEA), you are protected by the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since we do not collect personal identifiable data (like your name or email address) on our servers, we cannot directly identify you. However, you can control analytics and data collection policies through your device's built-in privacy settings. Purchases can be managed directly through your Google Play Store Account settings.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top of the policy. You are advised to review this page periodically for any updates.
